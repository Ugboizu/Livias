Frequently Asked Questions

The questions below are the frequently asked questions by customers of LHC



Does LHC grow virgin hair?

Our hair butter and hair growth oil used with proper hair care techniques reduces hair breakage and grows virgin hair


What is the hair oil for?

The hair oil is mostly for nourishing the scalp, preventing hair breakage and sealing in moisture. By nourishing the scalp, it promotes hair growth too and it also softens the hair.


Is LHC only for Virgin hair?

No, it is for both virgin and relaxed hair


Do you have a shampoo and conditioning set?

No, we only have hair butter, hair oil and spray bottles at the moment


What are the Spray bottles used for?

For applying water to hydrate the hair, you could mix your leave-in conditioner,water and hair oil in it, incase of combing or hair styling


When do I use the hair oil?

You can use your hair oil after you have applied your leave-in conditioner


How often should I use the hair oil?

You can use the hair oil at least 3 times in a week
